About E-SUMMIT


                E-SUMMIT is a three day entrepreneurship conclave, started as an initiative of E-Cell, IIT Bhubaneswar. It is an annual celebration of innovation, creativity and business awareness.


It serves as a platform for young entrepreneurs, innovators, and industry leaders to connect, collaborate, and learn from each other.


Our fest includes various engaging activities such as workshops, seminars, panel discussions, competitions, and, of course, the YESTA.
